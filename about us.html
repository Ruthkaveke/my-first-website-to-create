ABOUTS US

Nyali beach is a public beach found in mombasa around Nyali area

The beach was made public by the government on Kenya some times after getting independent

Its used by people from morning to evening at round six pm for security purposes

The above picture shows a small part of the beach

Its a non profit making organisation to enable kenyans enjoy the beauty of their country

A wonderful hotel called Nyali hotel is located along its shore making this place a nice place to be

WHY VISIT?

Its a beach with soft white sand and clean blue waters

Along its shore are smart costumes offered at a cheap price

The beach is under full security

Along the shore many activities occurs e.g football,bicycle riding and many activities

All are welcomed to enjoy the beauty of the blue waters
